Wolf

The name Wolf in English originates from the old english word Wulf.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus
  • Average Length: 105-160 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 8 -13 years
  • Habitat: Forests

The Wolf specializes in cooperative game hunting. The fur of the wolf is usually mottled white, brown, gray and black. Sometimes in Artic regions they may be nearly all white
